I am an artistic director who graduated from Penninghen in 2018, based in Paris. Specializing in the creation of visual identities and web design, my goal is to give meaning to the brands that call on me and to translate their vision into an inspired design designed to last.

Kindness and listening are for me the essential drivers in my artistic approaches. This quest for truth and sincerity is always at the heart of my work, because it is above all a common thread in my personal life. Sensitive, reactive, empathetic and demanding, I will know how to adapt and find the most accurate answers to your problems within the allotted time and in a good mood!
